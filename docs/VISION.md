# Portfolio Vision

## Purpose
The purpose of this portfolio is to provide a single, unified source of truth for all client projects, ensuring high standards of organization, code quality, and automation.

## Target Audience
- Clients: To show a professional, standardized body of work.
- Developers: To provide a consistent environment for collaboration.

## Quality Non-Negotiables
- Every repo must have a standardized structure (src, docs, tests).
- Every deployment must be logged in CHANGELOG.md.
- No secrets or keys in code.

## Success in 1 Year
A fully automated ecosystem where 10+ client sites are managed under one master repo with 100% uptime and zero "drift" in standards.
